About Me

Before I began studying computer science, I Graduated from Washington State University with a degree in Pre-Health Bioloy in the spring 2017. After working as an ICU CNA and a volunteer EMT for the next year and a half, I was accepted into Seattle Univerisites Doctorate of Nurse Practicitoner Program. During this time I suffered a back injury at the Hopsital and took an extended leave of absence. Since I no longer had applications, I spent my free time learning Javascript and HTML. I quickly realized my true calling and declined my offer to Seattle U. I began self teaching through Udemy, myfreecodecamp, and hackerRank. Once I learned of the University of Washington Web Development Bootcamp, I applied and was accepted to the summer cohort beginning may 20th. I am currently enrolled with an A Average and am looking for a full stack development job.
